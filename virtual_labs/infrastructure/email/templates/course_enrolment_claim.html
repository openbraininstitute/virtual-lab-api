What is Open Brain Institute?

OBI is the next generation platform that connects explorative features with a powerful set of model-building tools for conducting simulation experiments. From ion channel to whole-brain modeling, OBI supports neuroscientists in their research efforts. Explore, collect, and use real biological data to digitally simulate neurons.
